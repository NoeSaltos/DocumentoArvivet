	\chapter*{Abstract}
	
	\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}
	This project focuses on developing an integrated management system for "ARVIVET," a veterinary center with two branches in Guayaquil, which faces operational challenges due to the manual handling of appointments and medical records. The proposal includes a web platform for veterinary staff and a mobile application for clients, facilitating appointment scheduling, medical history consultation, and enhanced communication between the clinic and pet owners. This digital system automates reminders, centralizes data in a unified database, and improves the user experience. By implementing this solution, "ARVIVET" will position itself as an innovative veterinary center capable of delivering higher-quality care and operational efficiency.
